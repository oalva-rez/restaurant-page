OUR STORY

Familia Lizarraga

It all started with a coffee bean farmer in the late 1980's living in Jalisco, Mexico. Derived from their roots, Mystic Mocha brings its community a sense of hard work and dedication given by that same farmer in the 80's. One coffee cup at a time.

Three generations down, Israel Lizarraga strives to keep the family legacy alive. Providing an unchanged level of quality to those of their community.
